In a Nutshell

I started my career as an Aerospace Engineer working at Boeing. Over the course of eight years, I advanced from analyzing airplane performance to leading the certification project for Extended Operations (ETOPS) on the 737MAX line of airplanes. As much as I loved working with airplanes, I felt drawn to the expansive world of Tech. In 2017, I left Boeing and started my company, Prusik Development, to focus on the intersection between technology and the outdoor industry. I used Prusik Development to become a self-taught Python developer and to create Get Yr Beta, an open source web application to facilitate, automate, and coordinate planning logistics for outdoor adventures.

My objective is to combine my background as a project manager and engineer in the aviation industry with my programming and web development experience to complete my transition into the Tech industry.
